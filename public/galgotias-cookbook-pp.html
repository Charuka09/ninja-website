Privacy Policy


This Privacy Policy page covers what information we collect, store and how we use them when you use our Galgotias Cookbook Google Assistant App, the site galgotias-cookbook.appspot.com and this webpage.




What information we collect


Galgotias Cookbook app allows the you to connect with a an account for some of its invocations/actions. Connecting your account is only for storing your batch number and other minor details that are necessary for the app. We allow, as of last update to this privacy policy, only Google login and Facebook login, and collect the minimal most information which include email, name and public profile.
None of your info is sold/lend/given to a third party.
However, on viewing this website/webpage and the login web-app we may store optional cookies for logging data via Google Analytics.



How do we use the information


The conversation logs and other informations we collect from the app and website are solely for purpose of error detection and feature improvements. None of the information is sold/shared with any third party. You can view Google's Privacy Policy here.
